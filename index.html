Why We Need to Talk

Survey Highlights:

  • 75% began thinking about their bodies between ages 8–13
  • Triggers include: media influence, puberty, peer comparison
  • Over 70% often compare themselves to people online

What You Can Do

Schools

Promote diverse body image and curriculum changes.

Teachers

Use inclusive praise, avoid appearance-based comments.

Parents

Model self-love, be mindful of language.

Students

Encourage friends, avoid comparisons, share stories.

World Leaders

Regulate harmful media and support body diversity in law.
